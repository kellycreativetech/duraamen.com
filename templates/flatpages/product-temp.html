Perdüre A01 | Acrylic Sealer

Downloads
  • Technical Data Sheet
  • Architectural Document
  • Color Chart

Perdüre A01 is a clear acrylic solution formulated to create a clear, non-yellowing protective coating that provides a tough, UV and abrasion resistant film protecting surfaces from moisture penetration, staining, dirt, dust and wear. Perdüre A01 is an excellent choice for both interior and exterior applications.

Most commonly used as a sealer for Düraamen concrete, epoxy terrazzo and polymeric terrazzo flooring systems. It is also used for sealing old and new concrete floors, exterior ramps, decks, sidewalks, slate and exposed aggregate surfaces.

advantages
  • UV resistant g Excellent clarity & color retention
  • Low maintenance finish
  • Excellent abrasion & Mar resistance
  • Seals substrate from moisture penetration
